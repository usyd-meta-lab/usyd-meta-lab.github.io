Relational Monitoring

  • 1You will solve a series of reasoning problems
  • 2After each answer, rate your confidence that you were correct
  • 3Try to be as accurate as possible with your confidence — not just saying "100%" each time
  • 4Calibration matters: a good score means your confidence tracks your actual accuracy

What this measures

The Relational Monitoring Task assesses the accuracy of metacognitive monitoring for relational reasoning problems. It separates first-order performance from the ability to accurately track one's own performance.
